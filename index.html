Traditional fire safety training often falls short in preparing hotel staff for the complexities of real emergencies. With numerous guests and a complex layout, hotels pose unique challenges. Effective training is crucial to ensure staff can respond confidently during a fire emergency, prioritizing guest safety and minimizing potential damage. Standard procedures and drills provide a basic understanding but often fail to simulate the pressure and unpredictability of an actual fire, making scenario-based training vital for comprehensive preparation.
